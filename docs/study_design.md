# Study Design

## Research Questions

- What microbiome, multi-omics, dietary, lifestyle, and clinical factors are associated with individualized nutrition response?
- Which features improve prediction beyond baseline clinical and dietary covariates?
- How should model performance and interpretability be evaluated across cohorts or subgroups?

## Cohort Definition

Document inclusion criteria, exclusion criteria, recruitment setting, study timeline, and participant metadata.

## Data Modalities

- Microbiome profiles
- Metabolomics, proteomics, genomics, or other omics data
- Dietary intake records
- Clinical biomarkers
- Lifestyle and behavioral measures

## Outcomes

Define primary and secondary outcomes, including measurement windows and transformation rules.

## Analysis Plan

Document preprocessing, confounder handling, train/test splitting, validation strategy, and sensitivity analyses.
